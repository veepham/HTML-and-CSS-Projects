About Mup

Mup is a 10-12 year old Cocker Spaniel Terrier (Corkie) mix living in Portland, Oregon!
She was taken in from a shelter in Northern California to Oregon Dog Rescue, where she found her forever home on June 22, 2018.

What is a Corkie?
Corkie is a designer crossbreed, a mix between an American Cocker Spaniel and Yorkshire Terrier...Corkie is lively, mildly energetic and bubbly in temperament, and they also have jovial attitudes that make them fit into many different family arrangements.
